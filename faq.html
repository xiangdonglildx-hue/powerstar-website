FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our products

Installation

All our apps are available on Google Play Store. Search for the app name (e.g., "Thermometer" or "AI Photo Filters") or click the download links on our product pages.
Our apps require Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher. Check your device settings to see your current Android version.
Yes, all our apps are free to download and use. Some apps offer optional premium features through in-app purchases.

Using the Apps

Our Thermometer app uses your device's sensors to measure ambient temperature. For best results, keep your device at room temperature for a few minutes before taking a reading.
Yes! Our Voice Changer app supports real-time voice modification. You can use it during calls, gaming, or live streaming.
The Microphone app supports MP3, WAV, and AAC formats. You can choose your preferred format in the app settings.

Troubleshooting

Try these steps: 1) Clear the app cache in Settings > Apps, 2) Update to the latest version from Google Play, 3) Restart your device. If the problem persists, contact our support team.
Check your device settings: Go to Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Permissions, and ensure Microphone permission is enabled.

Billing & Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed through Google Play. Go to Google Play > Profile > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions to cancel or modify your subscription.
Refund requests are handled through Google Play's refund policy. Contact our support team if you need assistance with a refund request.
