Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does Voice Changer work with regular phone calls?

A: Yes! Voice Changer integrates seamlessly with your phone's calling system. Simply activate the app before or during a call, select your desired voice effect, and your voice will be transformed in real-time throughout the entire conversation. Works with both incoming and outgoing calls.

Q: Can I use Voice Changer with WhatsApp and other calling apps?

A: Absolutely! Voice Changer works with all major calling applications including WhatsApp, Telegram, Messenger, Zoom, Skype, and more. The app operates at the system level, so any app that uses your microphone will have your transformed voice.

Q: Will the other person know I'm using a voice changer?

A: Our advanced AI processing ensures natural-sounding voice transformations. The effects are designed to be convincing and realistic. However, for transparency and ethical use, we recommend informing close friends and family when using voice effects during personal calls.
