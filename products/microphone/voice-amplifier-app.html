Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is a voice amplifier app?

A: A voice amplifier app turns your smartphone into a sound booster device. It captures your voice through the phone's microphone and amplifies it through speakers or headphones, making it louder and clearer.

Q: How much can the app amplify my voice?

A: The Voice Amplifier app can boost your voice up to 200% of normal volume. Adjustable levels let you choose the right amplification for any situation—from soft enhancement to powerful amplification.

Q: Can I use this as a hearing amplifier?

A: Yes! The app includes hearing assistance functionality. Connect headphones and use it to boost quiet sounds for clearer conversations. Note: Not a certified medical hearing aid device.
