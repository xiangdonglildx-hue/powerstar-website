Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What Bluetooth devices can I connect to?

A: The Bluetooth Microphone app works with any Bluetooth-enabled audio device—speakers, headphones, sound systems, car audio, and even smart TVs. Just pair your phone and start speaking.

Q: How far does the Bluetooth microphone range work?

A: The Bluetooth microphone has a range of up to 10 meters (33 feet) in open space. Perfect for living rooms, conference rooms, classrooms, and small event spaces.

Q: Does it work with Bluetooth headphones?

A: Yes! You can use Bluetooth headphones as both input (some headphones have built-in mic) and output. Great for hearing assistance—boost quiet sounds directly to your headphones.
