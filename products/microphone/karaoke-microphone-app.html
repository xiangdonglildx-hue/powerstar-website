Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use this app for karaoke parties?

A: Yes! The Microphone app is perfect for karaoke parties. Connect to Bluetooth speakers, grab your phone, and sing your favorite songs. Works with any Bluetooth sound system.

Q: Does the app affect singing voice quality?

A: The app includes noise reduction technology that filters background noise while preserving your natural voice. Your singing will be clear and crisp through connected speakers.

Q: Can multiple phones connect to one speaker?

A: While Bluetooth typically connects one device at a time, you can easily switch between phones for group karaoke sessions. Pass the phone around for everyone to sing!
