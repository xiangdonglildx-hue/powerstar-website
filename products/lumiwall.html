Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Lumiwall app

What is Lumiwall - HD Wallpapers?

Lumiwall is an Android wallpapers app offering HD backgrounds with daily updates, smart search, and favorites collection. It has 500K+ downloads on Google Play and helps users personalize their devices.

Are the wallpapers really HD quality?

Yes, Lumiwall offers wallpapers in HD quality optimized for various screen resolutions. The app supports different display sizes from phones to tablets, ensuring perfect fit.

How often are new wallpapers added?

New wallpapers are added daily. Fresh content is automatically delivered to keep your screen looking new and exciting. You'll never run out of beautiful backgrounds.

Is Lumiwall wallpapers app free?

Yes, Lumiwall is free to download and use with ads. Optional in-app purchases are available for premium features and an ad-free experience.

Can I save my favorite wallpapers?

Yes, Lumiwall has a favorites feature where you can save and organize wallpapers you love. Add unlimited favorites and access them anytime from your personal collection.

What categories are available?

Lumiwall offers 20+ categories including Nature, Abstract, Animals, Art, Minimalist, Anime, Sports, Travel, and more. Browse by style, color, or theme to find exactly what you want.

Can I use my own photos as wallpapers?

Yes, Lumiwall supports custom photo import. You can use your own photos from your gallery as wallpapers. The app will optimize them for your screen size.

What Android version is required?

Lumiwall app requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or above. It's optimized to work smoothly on both older devices and the latest Android phones.
