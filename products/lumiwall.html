Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Lumiwall app

What is Lumiwall?

Lumiwall is an Android wallpaper app for browsing 4K, aesthetic, anime, live, nature, and other wallpaper styles.

Will every wallpaper fit every Android screen?

Wallpapers can fit devices differently. Cropping and display quality depend on the image, screen size, launcher, and Android wallpaper settings.

Which wallpaper styles can I explore?

You can explore 4K wallpapers, aesthetic wallpapers, anime wallpapers, live wallpapers, nature wallpapers, and other Android background styles.

Can I save favorite wallpapers?

Yes, Lumiwall includes a favorites feature so you can keep wallpapers you want to revisit.

Can I use my own photos as wallpapers?

Yes, Lumiwall supports custom photo import. Final display depends on the photo resolution, crop, screen size, and Android settings.

What Android version is required?

Lumiwall requires Android 5.0 or above. Feature availability and display behavior can vary by device and Android version.

Can I use wallpapers commercially?

Check the source and usage terms for each image before using it outside personal device personalization.

What Android version is required?

Lumiwall app requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or above. It's optimized to work smoothly on both older devices and the latest Android phones.
