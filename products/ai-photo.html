Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about AI Photo & Video Filters

What is AI Photo?

AI Photo is an Android photo editing app for trying AI filter styles such as anime, cartoon, aesthetic, vintage, selfie, pet, and social photo edits.

How predictable are AI filter results?

No. Results depend on the original photo, lighting, face or subject visibility, selected filter, processing quality, and export settings.

Can I use AI Photo for Instagram or TikTok content?

You can use the app to prepare social photo ideas and edited images, but audience response, reach, and platform behavior depend on factors outside the app.

Does AI Photo replace professional editing tools?

No. AI Photo is a consumer Android editing app for quick creative styles and references, not a replacement for professional retouching or design workflows.

Does it work offline?

Some basic features may work offline, while AI-powered filters can require an internet connection for processing.

What Android version is required?

AI Photo requires Android 5.0 or above. Feature availability and processing behavior can vary by device and app version.

How many AI filters are included?

The app includes 34+ AI-powered filters covering categories like dress-up (10+ outfits), holiday themes (8+ seasonal), professional styles, artistic effects, and one-tap beautification.

Can I share directly to social media?

Yes, the app has built-in sharing features. You can directly share your edited photos and videos to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and other social platforms with one tap.
