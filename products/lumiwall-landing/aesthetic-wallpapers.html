Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes a wallpaper 'aesthetic'?

A: Aesthetic wallpapers feature harmonious color palettes, artistic compositions, and mood-evoking imagery. They often use pastel colors, minimalist designs, vintage elements, or specific visual themes like cottagecore, dark academia, or vaporwave.

Q: Can I find pastel aesthetic wallpapers?

Q: Are there aesthetic wallpapers for dark mode?
