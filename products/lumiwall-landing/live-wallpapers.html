Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do live wallpapers drain battery?

A: Battery impact varies by animation, screen time, device, and power settings. Test the wallpaper on your own phone.

Q: Will live wallpapers work on my Android phone?

A: Yes! Live wallpapers work on Android 5.0+ devices. They're compatible with most phones including Samsung, Google Pixel, OnePlus, Xiaomi, and more. Check your phone's live wallpaper settings.

Q: How do I set a live wallpaper on Android?

A: Download Lumiwall, browse the live wallpapers category, tap your favorite, and select 'Set as Wallpaper'. Android will automatically recognize it as a live wallpaper and apply the animation.
