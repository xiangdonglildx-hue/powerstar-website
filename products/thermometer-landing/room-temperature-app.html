Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does this app read room temperature from my phone?

A: No. Thermometer uses your location and nearby weather data to provide a helpful room comfort reference on Android. Use a dedicated room thermometer or smart-home sensor when you need room-level readings.

Q: Why use a room temperature app?

A: Local temperature, humidity, and air pressure together give you a quick picture of nearby conditions, which can help with heating, cooling, airflow, and general comfort decisions.

Q: Is the room temperature app free?

A: Yes. Thermometer is free to download on Android.
