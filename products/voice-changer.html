Some gaming, streaming, or live audio setups may support voice effect preview depending on your phone, Android version, and audio routing. Results are not the same on every device.

Yes, the app includes built-in recording so you can save voice clips on your device. Recording quality and save behavior depend on your phone microphone, settings, and available storage.
