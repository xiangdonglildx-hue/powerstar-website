Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I check room temperature without a thermometer?

A: Yes! Our room temperature app uses your smartphone's built-in sensors to measure ambient temperature. No physical thermometer needed—just download the app and get instant readings.

Q: How accurate is the room temperature app?

A: The app provides temperature readings within 1-2°C accuracy. For best results, keep your phone at room temperature for a few minutes before measuring, and avoid holding it in your hand during measurement.

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Yes! The room temperature app works offline using your phone's built-in sensors. However, weather comparison features require an internet connection for accurate local weather data.
