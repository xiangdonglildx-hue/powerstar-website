Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the indoor thermometer app work without a sensor?

A: The app uses your phone's built-in battery temperature sensor combined with local weather data to estimate indoor temperature. For more accurate readings, it also uses humidity and pressure sensors available on most modern smartphones.

Q: Is the indoor thermometer app free?

A: Yes! The Thermometer app is completely free to download and use. All features including temperature monitoring, humidity tracking, and weather alerts are available at no cost.

Q: Can I monitor multiple rooms with this app?

A: Yes! You can save temperature readings for different locations and track temperature history. The app allows you to label and organize readings by room name for easy monitoring.
