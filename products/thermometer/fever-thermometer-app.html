Fever Temperature Guide

✅ Normal: 97-99°F (36.1-37.2°C)

Healthy body temperature range. Continue monitoring if recovering from illness.

⚠️ Low Fever: 99-100.4°F (37.2-38°C)

Mild elevation. Monitor closely, especially in children. May indicate onset of illness.

🚨 Fever: Above 100.4°F (38°C)

Medical attention may be needed. Track frequency, duration, and patterns. Contact doctor if persistent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use this app to measure body temperature?

A: This app is designed for tracking and logging body temperature readings from your thermometer. It helps you record temperatures, track fever patterns, and maintain health history. For actual measurement, use a medical thermometer and log the results in the app.

Q: How do I track fever for multiple family members?

A: The app allows you to create profiles for each family member. You can add children, parents, or anyone you need to monitor. Each profile keeps separate temperature logs and fever history, making it easy to track everyone's health.

Q: What temperature indicates a fever?

A: For adults, a fever is generally considered 100.4°F (38°C) or higher. For children, anything above 100°F (37.8°C) may indicate fever. The app highlights elevated temperatures and allows you to set custom thresholds based on your doctor's recommendations.
