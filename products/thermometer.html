Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Thermometer app

What is Thermometer - Hygrometer?

Thermometer - Hygrometer is a weather app for Android that provides real-time temperature, humidity, and air pressure readings using GPS-based weather data. It has 1M+ downloads and 3.8 rating on Google Play.

How accurate is the Thermometer app?

The Thermometer app uses GPS positioning to fetch weather data from nearby weather stations. It requires internet connection and GPS. Accuracy depends on local weather station data quality.

Can it measure indoor temperature?

No, the Thermometer app cannot measure indoor temperature directly. It uses external weather data based on GPS location to provide outdoor temperature readings.

Is Thermometer app free?

Yes, Thermometer - Hygrometer is free to download with ads. Optional in-app purchases are available for premium features like extended forecasts and location history.

Does the app work offline?

The app requires internet connection to fetch weather data from servers. GPS is also needed for location-based readings. Offline mode shows last cached data.

What Android version is required?

Thermometer app requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or above. It's optimized to work smoothly on both older devices and the latest Android phones.

What is the discomfort index?

The discomfort index calculates how comfortable the weather feels based on temperature and humidity combination. It helps you plan activities and dress appropriately for the conditions.

Can I use this for fishing?

Yes! The barometer feature is perfect for fishing enthusiasts. Atmospheric pressure changes can indicate fish activity levels, helping you choose the best fishing times and locations.
