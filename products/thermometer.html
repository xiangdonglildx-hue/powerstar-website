Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Thermometer app

What is Thermometer - Hygrometer?

Thermometer - Hygrometer is an Android weather app that shows local temperature, humidity, and air pressure using GPS-based weather data. Available readings depend on your location connection and nearby data sources.

How accurate is the Thermometer app?

The app uses GPS positioning to fetch nearby weather data. Accuracy depends on your location signal, internet connection, and the quality of nearby weather-station data.

Can it replace an indoor thermometer?

No. Thermometer uses GPS location and nearby weather data to provide a general environmental reference on Android. Use dedicated indoor, smart-home, medical, or professional weather instruments when you need device-specific readings.

Is Thermometer app free?

Yes, Thermometer - Hygrometer is free to download with ads. Optional in-app purchases are available for premium features like extended forecasts and location history.

Does the app work offline?

The app needs internet access to refresh weather data, and GPS is used for location-based readings. Availability may vary if your device is offline.

What Android version is required?

Thermometer app requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or above.

What is the discomfort index?

The discomfort index gives a simple comfort reference based on temperature and humidity, which may help with everyday planning and clothing decisions.

Can I use this for fishing?

Some people use air-pressure trends as one reference when planning fishing trips. The app can help you view pressure changes, but fishing conditions also depend on weather, location, season, and many other factors.
