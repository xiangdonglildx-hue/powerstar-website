Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Microphone app

What is Microphone - Voice Amplifier?

Microphone - Voice Amplifier is an Android app that turns your phone into a wireless microphone with voice amplification, noise reduction, and Bluetooth support. It has 5M+ downloads on Google Play and is trusted by users worldwide.

Can I use this for hearing assistance?

Yes, the app includes hearing amplifier functionality with noise reduction. However, it is not a certified medical hearing aid device. Consult a healthcare professional for medical hearing issues.

Does it work with Bluetooth speakers?

Yes, the app supports Bluetooth connectivity. You can connect to Bluetooth speakers or headsets for wireless audio transmission. Works with most Bluetooth devices on the market.

Is Microphone app free?

Yes, Microphone - Voice Amplifier is free to download with ads. Optional in-app purchases are available for premium features like custom themes and advanced noise reduction.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, the app works offline for voice amplification and recording features. Only ads and optional cloud features require internet connection.

What Android version is required?

Microphone app requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or above. It's optimized to work smoothly on both older devices and the latest Android phones.
