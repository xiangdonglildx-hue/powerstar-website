## Submission Checklist

### Before Submitting

1. **Prepare Media:**
   - Product thumbnail (630x400px PNG)
   - Gallery images (3-5 showing app features)
   - Optional: Demo video (30-60 seconds)

2. **Create Maker Profile:**
   - Personal Product Hunt account
   - Add bio with company info
   - Link to social profiles

3. **Timing:**
   - Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
   - Best time: 8-9 AM Pacific time
   - Avoid weekends and holidays

### Submission Day

1. **Submit at launch time** (8 AM PT)
2. **Write first comment** (maker story):
   ```
   Hi Product Hunt! 👋

   We built [Product Name] because [problem we solved].

   As a small team from Wuhan, China, we've been creating practical Android apps since 2017. Our mission is simple: make useful tools that don't require accounts, work offline, and respect privacy.

   [Product Name] now has [X million] downloads because it solves a real problem: [problem].

   Key differentiator: [what makes us unique]

   Happy to answer questions!
   ```

3. **Engage throughout the day:**
   - Respond to comments quickly
   - Thank upvoters
   - Answer questions transparently

### After Launch

1. **Share on social media**
2. **Update website with Product Hunt badge**
3. **Collect feedback for improvements**

## Maker Story Templates

### Thermometer Maker Story

```
Hi Product Hunt! 👋

We built Thermometer - Hygrometer because we noticed a common misconception: many "thermometer apps" claim to measure room temperature using phone sensors—but those sensors only measure phone internal temperature (CPU/battery heat), not ambient temperature.

Our approach is different: we use GPS positioning to fetch accurate local weather data from nearby stations. This gives real ambient temperature, humidity, and barometric pressure—perfect for fishing enthusiasts (barometer predicts fish activity!), health monitoring, and weather planning.

The app has grown to 1M+ downloads organically, with users praising the barometer feature for fishing trips.

Happy to answer questions about weather apps, fishing tips, or building privacy-first tools!
```
