Tips for Best Anime Results

📸 Use Clear Photos

High-quality input photos produce better anime transformations. Avoid blurry or dark images.

👤 Face the Camera

Front-facing portraits work best for anime style. Side profiles may produce uneven results.

💇 Simple Backgrounds

Plain or simple backgrounds help the AI focus on transforming the subject.

👗 Consider Outfits

Some anime styles include clothing changes — choose photos where your outfit works well.

Anime Styles Explained

Classic Anime (Shonen/Shojo)

The most popular style featuring large eyes, small mouths, and vibrant colors. Works well for portraits and selfies. Similar to popular anime series like Naruto, One Piece, or Sailor Moon.

Manga Style

Black and white comic book style with dramatic shading. Perfect for creating manga-style profile pictures or comic panels.

Kawaii/Cute Style

Extra cute with bigger eyes, softer features, and pastel colors. Popular for cute social media avatars and profile pictures.

Ghibli-Style

Soft, painterly style inspired by Studio Ghibli films. Creates dreamy, nostalgic artwork from landscape and portrait photos.
