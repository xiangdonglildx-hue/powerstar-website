What Are AI Photo Filters?

AI photo filters use machine learning algorithms to analyze and enhance images. Unlike traditional filters that apply uniform effects, AI filters adapt to each photo's unique characteristics—detecting faces, objects, lighting, and composition.

Types of AI Photo Filters

1. Style Transfer Filters

Transform your photos into artwork inspired by famous painters, cinematic styles, or vintage aesthetics.

2. Portrait Enhancement

AI-powered skin smoothing, eye brightening, and facial contouring for professional-looking portraits.

3. Scene Enhancement

Intelligent adjustment of colors, contrast, and exposure based on scene type (landscape, food, architecture).

4. Creative AI Effects

Unique features like AI dress-up, background replacement, and artistic transformations.
