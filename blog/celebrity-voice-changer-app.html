How Celebrity Voice Changers Work

Voice changer apps analyze several voice characteristics:

  • Pitch — How high or low the voice sounds
  • Timbre — The unique quality that makes voices distinct
  • Tempo — Speaking speed and rhythm

AI models trained on thousands of voice samples learn to modify these parameters to create different voice styles.
