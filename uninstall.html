Uninstall Our Apps

Follow these steps to remove Power Star Apps from your device

How to Uninstall

1

Open Settings

Go to your Android device Settings and tap on "Apps" or "Applications".

2

Find the App

Scroll through the list to find the Power Star App you want to uninstall (Thermometer, Microphone, Voice Changer, Lumiwall, or AI Photo Filters).

3

Tap Uninstall

Tap on the app name, then tap "Uninstall" and confirm your choice.

4

Alternative: Long Press

You can also long-press the app icon on your home screen and drag it to "Uninstall" or tap "App info" then "Uninstall".

Need Help?

If you're having trouble uninstalling or have questions, contact our support team.
