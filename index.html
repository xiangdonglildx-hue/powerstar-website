Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our apps

Are Power Star Apps free?

Yes, all our apps are free to download and use with ads. Premium features are available through optional in-app purchases for users who want enhanced functionality.

Do you collect personal data?

We only collect data necessary for app functionality. We don't sell your data to third parties. All data handling complies with GDPR and privacy regulations.

Where can I download the apps?

All apps are available on Google Play Store. Search "Power Star Apps" or use the download links on each app's page. We're expanding to other platforms soon.

How do I get support?

Visit our Help Center for guides and FAQs, or email us at support@powerstarapps.com. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Do the apps work offline?

Most features work offline. Apps like Thermometer use device sensors, while AI Photo Filters process images locally. Some features may require internet for updates.

Are there in-app purchases?

Yes, optional premium features are available. Premium removes ads and unlocks advanced functionality. All core features remain free forever.

What Android versions are supported?

All apps support Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and above. We optimize for both older and newer devices to ensure broad compatibility across 95%+ Android phones.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love user feedback. Email your suggestions to feedback@powerstarapps.com or leave a review on Google Play. Many features came from user requests.
